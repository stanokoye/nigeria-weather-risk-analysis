# Data Sources & Citations

All data used in this project is documented below. Each source is noted with its type, access date, and how it was used in the analysis.

---

## Primary Climate Data

### WeatherSpark — Abagana Climate Averages
- **URL:** https://weatherspark.com/y/52961/Average-Weather-in-Abagana-Nigeria-Year-Round
- **Type:** 30-year historical climate averages
- **Used for:** Monthly temperature (high/low), rainfall totals, rainy days, sunshine hours, humidity
- **Coverage:** Full year, Abagana, Anambra State
- **Accessed:** April 2026

### NASA POWER (Prediction of Worldwide Energy Resources)
- **URL:** https://power.larc.nasa.gov/
- **Type:** Satellite-derived climate parameters
- **Used for:** Daily climate data download for analysis
- **Coverage:** Global, daily resolution
- **Coordinates:** 6.184°N, 6.977°E (Abagana)

### CHIRPS (Climate Hazards Group InfraRed Precipitation with Station data)
- **URL:** https://www.chc.ucsb.edu/data/chirps
- **Type:** High-resolution rainfall estimates
- **Used for:** Historical rainfall validation
- **Coverage:** Africa, 0.05° resolution

## Official Meteorological Sources

### Nigerian Meteorological Agency (NiMet) — Seasonal Climate Predictions
- **URL:** https://nimet.gov.ng/scp
- **Type:** Annual seasonal forecast reports
- **Used for:** Little Dry Season (August break) predictions 2020–2026, Anambra-specific dry spell severity
- **Years reviewed:** 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, 2026

## Peer-Reviewed Research

### Springer / Climatic Change (2010)
- **Title:** West African monsoon: Is the August break "breaking" in the eastern humid zone of Southern Nigeria?
- **URL:** https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10584-009-9780-2
- **Used for:** Evidence that the August break is weakening in eastern Nigeria; historical LDS analysis 1983–2003

### World Water Policy / Wiley (2023)
- **Title:** Rainfall extremes and trends during the little dry season in Nigeria
- **URL:** https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/wwp2.12158
- **Used for:** LDS variability and reliability analysis

### ResearchGate / Himalayan Journal of Agriculture
- **Title:** Analysis of Rainfall Distributions and Variations in Nigeria
- **URL:** https://himjournals.com/hja/955/1189/articleID=1298/
- **Used for:** State-level rainfall distribution data including Anambra

### ResearchGate (2020)
- **Title:** Projected near-future changes in precipitation extremes over Anambra-Imo River Basin (CMIP6 HighResMIP)
- **Used for:** Climate change projections for Anambra-Imo Basin
