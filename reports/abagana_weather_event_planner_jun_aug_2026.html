Event planning essentials
🏗 Venue: Covered marquee tents or a hall are non-negotiable. Avoid bare soil — use interlock, concrete, or raised flooring. Proper drainage is critical in Anambra.
🔊 Technical setup: Weatherproof all sound systems and power supply. Use elevated cabling — surface flooding risk is real during heavy spells.
📋 Programme: Build a "rain delay buffer" of 30–45 min into the schedule. Pre-identify indoor fallback segments that can swap in without disrupting the flow.
🌡 Comfort: Humidity will be 75–90% throughout. Plan adequate ventilation and fans. Guests arriving from drier regions may find the heat index heavier than the temperature suggests.
📡 Monitor closely: 7–10 days before, check Nigerian Meteorological Agency (NiMet) forecasts for the specific week. Seasonal patterns are reliable; exact timing of storms is only predictable within ~1 week.
